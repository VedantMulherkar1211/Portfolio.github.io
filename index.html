About Me

Hello, I am Vedant Dharmendra Mulherkar, a computer engineering graduate from Mumbai Education Trust BKC Institution of Engineering in Nashik, under the University of Pune. I am proud to have achieved a First Class Distinction in 2022.

My educational journey began in Nashik, where I completed my secondary schooling and higher secondary schooling. In 2022, during my college years, I had the opportunity to undergo a corporate internship at Persistent Systems in Pune.

After that I join persistent as full time employee where I work on Data Integration project and my client was Kantar Media.

In addition, I was selected for the Zensar EDS program in 2021. This program provided me with an excellent platform to enhance my programming techniques and broaden my understanding of various tools. I possess a diverse set of skills, including Java core, SQL, Linux, and Git. Moreover, I have a keen interest in continuously expanding my knowledge and keeping up with the latest advancements in the industry. Outside of academics and work, I find joy in cycling, traveling, and engaging in content creation related to teaching and video editing. These hobbies allow me to explore my creative side and express my passion for sharing knowledge. Throughout my educational journey, I have achieved several certifications, including the MS Azure Fundamentals 900. Additionally, I have actively participated in various hackathons, one of which was organized by the Indian Army and focused on cybersecurity and machine learning. I am an enthusiastic learner who is always eager to embrace new technologies and concepts. Equally important to me is the opportunity to share my knowledge and contribute to the growth of others. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I am excited to embark on new challenges and make meaningful contributions in the field of computer engineering.
